FAQ

Questions, answered

Do I need any hardware to start?

No. Camera-Only mode runs on any smartphone — movement, speed, jump, agility, posture and best-position estimates with zero hardware. Sensors and lab equipment are optional and added in the Hybrid and Elite tiers as you grow.

Is camera AI as accurate as force plates and lab testing?

They do different jobs. Camera AI provides movement estimation from video; professional hardware provides ground-truth physical and physiological measurement. PlayerDNA combines both on one profile — estimates first, hardware confirms — so you start fast and validate when it matters.

Is this a medical or diagnostic tool?

No. PlayerDNA provides sport-performance analysis, position-suitability estimation and training guidance. Injury-risk outputs are indicators that prompt professional review — never a medical diagnosis. For pain, abnormal movement or return-to-play decisions, consult a qualified physician, physiotherapist or coach.

What ages does it support?

From grassroots youth to professionals. For players aged 6–16 a dedicated youth mode switches off adult-style judgment in favour of healthy development, coordination and a long-term pathway, with a parent-friendly summary.

Can I export the data and connect my own systems?

Yes. Reports export to PDF, and data exports to CSV/JSON. The Elite Ground-Truth tier adds API access so you can feed the validated athlete profile into your existing performance, medical and recruitment systems.

How is player and athlete data protected?

Athlete data is stored securely and used only to produce that player's profile and reports. Youth profiles require guardian consent, and you control who in your organization can access each athlete. Connect your own backend to keep data fully under your governance.

Frequently asked questions

How the first AI sports academy works, and how AI football position analysis turns one video into a full athlete profile.

What is PlayerDNA Labs?

PlayerDNA Labs is the first AI sports academy. It turns a single training or match video into a complete athlete profile, measuring speed, power, stamina, agility, biomechanics and injury risk across 16 dimensions, then identifies the football positions a player is naturally built for.

How does AI football position analysis work?

PlayerDNA's AI tracks an athlete's movement, sprint mechanics and body metrics from ordinary video, scores them against validated sports-science standards, and maps the results to the physical demands of each football position — producing a data-backed recommendation of the roles where a player is most likely to excel.

What do I need to get an athlete profile?

You only need one training or match video. PlayerDNA Labs analyses the footage and returns a full 16-dimension report covering speed and power, agility and endurance, strength and movement, readiness, and body and mind — with no wearables or lab equipment required.

Who is PlayerDNA Labs for?

PlayerDNA Labs is built for players who want to understand their potential, coaches and academies running talent identification, and scouts who need objective, comparable athlete data. It supports AI player development, recruitment and injury-risk awareness.

Why is PlayerDNA called the first AI sports academy?

PlayerDNA Labs combines automated, video-based athlete assessment with player development and certification in a single AI platform, rather than offering analytics or coaching in isolation — which is why it positions itself as the first AI sports academy.

How accurate are PlayerDNA's measurements?

Every PlayerDNA score maps to a validated testing protocol — EUROFIT sprint norms, Illinois Agility, CMJ vertical-jump norms, Yo-Yo/MSFT VO2max and ACSM strength tables — so results are calibrated to real standards and comparable between athletes.
